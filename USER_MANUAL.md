## ðŸš¦ Daily Workflow

### 1. Starting a Shift

- Navigate to the **Shifts** page.
- Click **"Open New Shift"**.
- Ensure the initial nozzle readings match the previous shift's closing readings.

### 2. Recording Sales

- Throughout the shift, use the **Sales** page to record transactions.
- Choose **Cash** or **Credit**.
- For Credit sales, specify the customer or vehicle details in the description.

### 3. Closing a Shift

- Navigate back to the **Shifts** page.
- Enter the **Current Reading** for all nozzles.
- The system will automatically calculate total liters sold and the expected cash/credit balance.

### 4. Managing Inventory

- Use the **Inventory** page to monitor fuel levels in Real-time.
- **Physical Dip**: Every few days, take a physical measurement of the tanks and record it via the "Physical Dip" button to adjust for temperature or evaporation variance.
- **Stock In**: Record purchases/tanker arrivals via the "Record Purchase" button to update your inventory and accounting records.
